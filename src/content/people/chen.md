I am a research assistant professor at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen) and Shenzhen Loop Area Institute (SLAI) starting in September 2025. I received my Ph.D. in the [Algorithms & Complexity Group](https://algcomp.uwaterloo.ca/) of [University of Waterloo](https://uwaterloo.ca/). I am fortunate to be advised by [Richard Peng](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~yangp/). Prior to this, I received my Master at [Georgia Institute of Technology](https://www.gatech.edu/) and B.Eng (Honors) at [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/) under supervision of [Can Wang](https://person.zju.edu.cn/wangcan).

My research revolves around the design, analysis, and implementation of provably efficient algorithms and data structures, with a focus on graphs. I am currently exploring research involving AI. I am a founding member and coordinator of the newly established [SDS CS Theory Group](https://sds-theory.github.io/).
