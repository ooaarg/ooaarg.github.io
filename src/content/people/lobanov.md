My research interests include Stochastic Optimization, Machine Learning, Federated Optimization, Gradient-Free Algorithms, etc. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. at the MIPT, Russia in the Laboratory of Advanced Combinatorics and Network Applications, supervised by Alexander Gasnikov. During my PhD I also spent some time at MBZUAI (ML Department at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence).
