I am Senior Research Associate at the Institute of Informatics Systems of Siberian Division of Russian Academy of Sciences.

My research interests concern the use of Logic in Computer Science and include Model Theory, Computability Theory, and Complexity Theory. I am particularly interested in novel directions in Knowledge Engineering and Artificial Intelligence that can benefit from applications of these fields of Mathematics.

My current research activities are concerned with hybrid approaches to AI, theory and practice of database systems, and AI tools for databases.
